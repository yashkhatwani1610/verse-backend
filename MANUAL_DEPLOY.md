## Step 2: Create Web Service Manually

1. After login, click **"New +"** button (top right)
2. Select **"Web Service"**
3. Scroll down and click **"Build and deploy from a Git repository"**
4. Click **"Connect account"** 
5. **IMPORTANT**: Choose **"Use existing repository"** or **"Public repository"**
